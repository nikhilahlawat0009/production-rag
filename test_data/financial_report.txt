Financial Report Q1 2024

Executive Summary
The company achieved record revenue of $500M in Q1 2024, representing a 25% year-over-year growth. 
Operating margins expanded to 18% from 15% in Q1 2023, driven by operational efficiency improvements.

Revenue Breakdown
- Product sales: $350M (70% of total)
- Service revenue: $150M (30% of total)

Market Analysis
The UK financial services market continues to show strong growth. Interest rates are expected to stabilize 
around 4.5% by mid-2024. Companies with strong balance sheets are positioned well for the expanding credit market.

Risk Assessment
Regulatory changes in the FCA's open banking requirements could impact third-party integrations. 
However, our modular architecture allows for rapid compliance updates.
